Lucas is dying. With a terminal cancer diagnosis and only months to live, he endures the ultimate humiliation: his wildly successful wife, Emily, is publicly flaunting her young lover in front of him and all of high society. While she tries to provoke his jealousy, he remains silent, hiding his devastating secret. This is a heart-wrenching story of sacrifice, asking how far a man will go to protect the woman he loves, even as she becomes the source of his greatest pain. Discover the shocking truth behind his quiet suffering.
